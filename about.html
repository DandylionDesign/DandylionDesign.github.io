About Dandelion design

Dandelion design was launched in 2020. Limited to working from home due during lockdown the company was created in a shed built from recycled materails. As dandelions are have an ability to thrive under the most adverse conditions and represent persistence transformation and survival it was an apt name for a company emerging out of a pandemic. The seeds’ journey illustrates a time of letting go, of starting something new and so the story begins.

About the artist

Andrea is a designer and craft person, who works in glass (on its own, and sometimes combined with other materials) to create handmade fused glass pieces. Over the years Andrea has experimented with many materials and enjoyed the endless possibilities there are in combining textures she has grown to love. Whether its timber, metal, leather, flint, plaster or something more obscure; there is a distinctive relationship she has with them all; and a respect for their limitations and potential. However she is now fully immersed in a love affair glass.

'I’ve always loved glass, playing with it's beautiful alchemy and three-dimensional depth to colour. There's so much to learn and so many ideas I want to put into practice, that I think it's safe to assume my kiln will have a long and hard life.'
